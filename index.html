About Us

Who We Are

865 ELITE FLAG FOOTBALL is a competitive flag football league dedicated to excellence, teamwork, and fun. Since our founding in 2025, we've been committed to providing an exciting flag football experience for our community.

Our Mission

To promote athletic excellence, build strong friendships, and experience the thrill of flag football competition. We believe in sportsmanship, dedication, and celebrating every victory together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Team registration is $500 per season. Free agent placement is $50 per player. Fees cover all league games, certified referees, and end-of-season awards.

Every team is guaranteed a minimum of 8 regular season games, plus the opportunity to compete in the playoffs.

Games are held at John Tarleton Park in Knoxville, Tennessee 37919.

Free agents are matched to teams based on position needs and team balance. When you sign up, you'll provide your preferred offensive and defensive positions. League administrators will place you before the season starts.

Yes! Every game has official league referees to ensure fair play and proper rule enforcement.

Championship trophies are awarded to the winning team. Individual awards include League MVP and Player of the Year.
